title: "Profile menu (admin)"

---

Open **Profile** in the app. If your role is **ADMIN**, you see the items below in addition to **Reports** and **Logout**.

## Opportunity management

**Profile** → **Opportunity management**

- Lists **manual opportunities** created in the app
- Tap a row to open opportunity details (admin view)
- **Create opportunity** — add a manual job (address, customer, assign rep)
- Edit or delete manual opportunities from the list

Use this when ops needs a job in the app that did not appear from the normal appointment feed. Sales reps do not have this screen.

## Statistics & Analytics

**Profile** → **Statistics & Analytics**

Monitor team activity across users:

1. **Users** — pick a rep to see their opportunities, surveys, and calculators
2. **Opportunities** — drill into a specific job
3. **Details** — survey completion, calculator progress, last activity

Useful for checking whether a rep has started or finished survey/calculator work on a job.

## Reports

**Profile** → **Reports**

Admins can:

- View KPIs across **all users** (not just your own)
- Filter by **rep**
- Export CSV
- See sold appointments for all users, appointment customers by rep

<Warning>
**Reports is in beta** — numbers may not be accurate. Use for direction only, not official commission or HR decisions.
</Warning>

Sales reps see the same Reports screen but **only their own data**, with no rep filter.

## Appointment cycle time

**Profile** → **Appointment cycle time**

- Filter by date range and **rep**
- See average, median, and p90 appointment duration
- Breakdown by end reason: contract signed (won), quoted, 2h timeout
- Per-record list of completed appointment cycles

Use this to review how long reps spend on site and whether jobs are finishing as Won or Quote.

## Admin Panel

**Profile** → **Admin Panel**

Central admin hub with tabs:

### Users

- List, search, and filter app users
- Create users (username, email, role, password)
- Edit user details, role, status
- Delete users (with confirmation)
- Link users to CRM assignees where configured

### CRM / GHL

- See which users have a CRM user ID linked
- Assign or update CRM user mapping for appointment routing

### Settings

- System settings (e.g. calculator toggles, step navigation, feature flags)
- Change values that affect what reps see in the app

### Win / loss

- Admin win/loss dashboard across the team
- Complements rep-level Dashboard stats

## Tools

**Profile** → **Tools**

Fill **survey placeholder images** for testing or unblocking a job:

1. Enter the **opportunity ID** (CRM or manual ID)
2. Optional: **Skip energy bill** fields when filling placeholders
3. Tap to fill — uploads placeholder photos to required survey image fields

Does not replace a real survey. Use for QA, demos, or recovery when ops approves it.

## Logout

Same as sales reps — signs you out of the app.

| Area | Sales rep | Admin |

| Progress tab title | **My Progress** | **All Progress** (everyone's jobs) |
