**Profile** → **Workflow Override**

Use when a rep is stuck, steps need marking complete, or survey images need placeholders — without asking the rep to reset the whole job.

## Load an opportunity

1. Enter the **opportunity ID** (CRM ID or manual opportunity ID)
2. Load the overview — you see workflow steps, survey status, calculator type, and image field counts

If no workflow exists yet, use **Ensure workflow** to create progress for that ID.

## Workflow steps

The overview lists all backend steps with status (pending, in progress, completed).

For each step you can **mark complete** after confirming — use when the work was done outside the app or the step is stuck.

Common actions:

| Action | When to use |
|--------|-------------|
| **Mark survey complete** | Survey is done but step 1 did not close — sets survey approved and completes Survey step |
| **Mark calculator complete** | Calculator finished but step 3 stuck — uses current calculator type (e.g. off-peak) |
| **Mark disclaimer complete** | Energy bill disclaimer signed but step did not update |
| **Complete step** (per step) | Any individual step needs forcing complete |

## Survey images

Overview shows each image field: count vs minimum required.

- **Fill all placeholders** — upload placeholder images to every required field (optional skip energy bill fields)
- **Fill one field** — target a single missing field

After filling, reload the overview and confirm **all images satisfied** before marking survey complete.

## Disclaimer display override

When the customer has no energy bill, control whether the **Energy Bill Disclaimer** step appears:

| Mode | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Auto** | App decides from survey answer |
| **Show** | Force disclaimer step visible |
| **Hide** | Force disclaimer step hidden |

Use carefully — hiding when a disclaimer is legally required can cause compliance issues.

## Calculator overview

Shows calculator type in progress (e.g. off-peak, flux) and current calculator step. Use **Mark calculator complete** when pricing is done but the workflow did not advance.

## Safety

- Confirm every destructive or complete action — changes affect the live opportunity
- Prefer fixing the specific step over resetting the whole workflow (see [Solar Progress admin tools](/admin/solar-progress) for full reset)
- Tell the rep when you override so they refresh **Solar Progress**
