The **Solar workflow** screen is the control centre for a single appointment. Steps unlock in order as you complete each one.

## Opening the workflow

From an opportunity, tap **Solar workflow**. You will see:

- Customer name and address
- Current step highlighted
- Progress through the full pipeline
- Job status: **In progress**, **Won**, or **Quoted**

## Core workflow steps

Exact steps can vary by opportunity type and admin settings, but most appointments follow this pattern:

### 1. Customer details & consent

Confirm contact details and collect early consent where required before design work.

### 2. Survey

Complete the site survey:

- Property photos (roof, fuse board, access, shading)
- Energy usage and bill information
- Notes on installation constraints

Save progress as you go — the survey autosaves. Submit when all required fields and photos are complete.

<Note>
If the customer **does not have an energy bill**, update the survey accordingly. The workflow may add an **Energy Bill Disclaimer** signing step after you save.
</Note>

### 3. Calculator & system design

Use the **Off-peak calculator** to model the system. Enter array sizes, battery options, and consumption details. Progress is saved per opportunity.

You do not need to choose a calculator type — the app uses Off-peak for all current appointments.

### 4. Pricing

Review generated pricing after the calculator. Admins may apply pricing overrides — if pricing looks wrong, contact operations before presenting to the customer.

### 5. Presentation

Deliver the sales presentation or **video presentation** to walk the customer through the system and savings.

### 6. OpenSolar (when used)

Some jobs link to **OpenSolar** for 3D design. Open the design from the workflow step and confirm the project ID is linked.

### 7. Signing steps

Depending on the job, you may need:

- Energy bill disclaimer
- Express consent
- Contract generation and signing
- Booking confirmation
- Email confirmation

Each signing step opens the signing provider in-app or in the browser. Wait for **signed** status before moving on.

### 8. Payment & installation booking

Complete payment collection and installation booking steps when they appear in your workflow.

### 9. Finish appointment

Use **Finish appointment** to record the outcome:

- **Won** — the customer committed and signed (or is proceeding to install)
- **Quoted** — you left a quote with the customer; they have not committed yet

These are the only two outcomes reps record. There is no separate "lost" option in the app.

## Outcome tracking

When you mark **Won** or **Quoted**, your dashboard and reports update. Outcomes are tracked from the date tracking was enabled — historical jobs before that date may not appear in performance charts.

## Admin-only actions

Admins can reset workflow steps, override pricing, and jump between steps. Standard reps should follow steps in order unless operations instructs otherwise.
