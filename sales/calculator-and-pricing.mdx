## Off-peak calculator

All current appointments use the **Off-peak calculator**. When the workflow reaches the calculator step, you go straight into Off-peak — there is no calculator type to pick.

The Off-peak path models standard domestic solar and battery quotes using current tariff assumptions configured by operations.

## Calculator inputs

Typical inputs include:

- Panel count and layout (solar arrays)
- Battery size and manufacturer options
- Annual consumption and export assumptions
- Tariff rates (current or projected)

The app validates required fields before you can proceed. Red or highlighted fields must be completed.

## Saving progress

Calculator progress is saved per opportunity. You can leave and return later — use **Progress** to find the job and continue where you left off.

## Pricing screen

After the calculator completes, open **Pricing** to review:

- System cost breakdown
- Finance options (if configured)
- Final figures to present to the customer

Do not screenshot or share draft pricing externally until the numbers are confirmed.

## Pricing overrides

Only admins can apply pricing overrides from the workflow tools menu. If you need a different price:

1. Note the opportunity ID
2. Contact your manager or operations
3. Wait for override confirmation before presenting updated pricing

## OpenSolar integration

Some opportunities use OpenSolar for design. The workflow links your OpenSolar project — open the design URL from the workflow step and ensure the correct project is associated before running the calculator.

## Tips

- Double-check consumption figures against the bill or customer estimate
- Confirm battery option matches what was quoted on the phone
- Re-run the calculator if survey findings change array size or shading assumptions
