## Log in

1. Go to [app.creativuk.co.uk](https://app.creativuk.co.uk)
2. Sign in with the username and password provided by your manager
3. If you forget your password, use **Forgot password** on the login screen

Stay signed in on your tablet or laptop for the day — you will be logged out automatically if your session expires.

## Main navigation

The bottom tabs are your home base:

| Tab | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| **Dashboard** | Your performance stats for the current month |
| **Opportunities** | Appointments and leads assigned to you |
| **Progress** | All active workflows and their current step |
| **Profile** | Settings, statistics, and support links |

## Opening an opportunity

From **Opportunities**:

1. Tap an appointment to see customer details
2. Tap **Start workflow** or **Continue workflow** to open the solar workflow screen

From **Progress**:

1. Search by customer name, postcode, or stage
2. Tap a row to jump straight into that job's workflow

## Before you leave for an appointment

- Confirm the opportunity appears in your list with the correct address and time
- Check you have network access at the property (mobile hotspot if needed)
- Charge your device — survey steps require camera and photo uploads

## Roles

Most sales reps use the standard user role. **Admin** users also see opportunity management, admin panel, and system tools — ignore those unless your role includes them.
