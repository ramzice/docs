On **Solar Progress**, the icons at the top right give you extra actions during an appointment.

## Won and Quote (header)

While the job shows **⏳ In Progress**, two buttons appear:

| Button | Use when |
|--------|----------|
| **Won** | Customer is proceeding (see [Hometree process](/sales/hometree) for HT timing) |
| **Quote** | Customer wants a quote only — not proceeding today |

After you tap one, the badge shows **✓ Won** or **✗ Quote**.

To change your mind before finishing, tap the small **refresh** button next to the badge to reset the status back to **In Progress**.

You will also choose **Won** or **Quote** on **Finish Appointment** at the end — that step organizes files into the correct folder.

## Tools — override a price

Tap the **wrench (Tools)** icon.

1. Modal opens: **Tools** → **Override a Price**
2. Select the calculator line if more than one is listed
3. Enter the new price
4. Tap **Apply Override**

Use this when operations or your team lead has agreed a different price during the appointment. Re-check **System Pricing** after applying.

## Reset progress

Tap the **refresh (Reset Progress)** icon (separate from the status reset above).

- Confirms: **Reset Progress** → **Yes, Reset Progress**
- Clears workflow progress for this appointment so you can start steps again

<Warning>
Only reset if you are sure — you may need to re-do survey, calculator, or signing steps. Ask your team lead if unsure.
</Warning>

## Starting a new calculator run

There is no separate "new calculator" button. To run the calculator again:

1. Go back to the **Calculate** step on Solar Progress, or  
2. Use **Reset Progress** if you need a full restart from the survey  

Calculator progress autosaves — you can usually reopen **Calculate** and adjust inputs without a full reset.

## Finish Appointment — organize files

After **Send Welcome Email**, **Finish Appointment** asks for **Won** or **Quote** again and runs **Confirm & Organize Files**.

| Outcome | Folder |
|---------|--------|
| **Won** | Customer Orders 2 |
| **Quote** | Customer Quotations |

Wait for "Organizing files..." to finish before leaving the appointment.

## Reports (Profile tab)

From **Profile** → **Reports** you can view your performance reports. Confirm when prompted to open the reports screen.

## What is not in the app for reps

These are handled by the office — you do not need to use or know about them:

- Creating appointments in external systems  
- Linking jobs from other software  
- Admin panels, user management, or workflow override screens  

If an appointment is missing from **Appointments**, contact your team lead — do not try to create duplicate jobs yourself unless you have been trained to.
