The Creativ Solar app is your day-to-day tool for running home solar appointments: from opening an opportunity through survey, pricing, presentation, and contract signing.

## Who this guide is for

Sales reps and field consultants who:

- Run in-home or remote solar appointments
- Use the Creativ Solar web app at [app.creativuk.co.uk](https://app.creativuk.co.uk)
- Need step-by-step help on workflows, calculator, and signing

## What you can do in the app

| Area | What it covers |
|------|----------------|
| **Dashboard** | Your monthly appointments, sales won, and conversion rate |
| **Opportunities** | Appointments assigned to you, with customer details |
| **Progress** | Active workflows — see where each job is in the pipeline |
| **Solar workflow** | Step-by-step appointment flow for a single opportunity |
| **Calculator & pricing** | Off-peak calculator with system design inputs |
| **Survey** | Site survey photos and property details |
| **Signing** | Disclaimers, express consent, contracts, and booking confirmation |

## Typical appointment flow

1. Open the opportunity from **Opportunities** or **Progress**
2. Start or continue the **Solar workflow**
3. Complete the **survey** (photos and property details)
4. Run the **Off-peak calculator** and review **pricing**
5. Deliver the **presentation** or video presentation
6. Collect **signatures** (disclaimer, consent, contract)
7. Mark the job **Won** or **Quoted** when finished
