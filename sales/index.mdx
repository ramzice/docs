This guide covers **only the app** — how to log in, run an appointment, and finish a sale. It does not cover back-office systems.

## Quick start

1. Open [app.creativuk.co.uk](https://app.creativuk.co.uk) and log in
2. Go to **Appointments** and select today's appointment
3. Check customer details, then tap **Start Solar Progress**
4. Work through each step on **Solar Progress** until the job is complete

## Outcomes in the app

You only record two results:

| Button in app | Meaning |
|---------------|---------|
| **Won** | Customer is proceeding — files go to Customer Orders |
| **Quote** | Customer took a quote only — files go to Customer Quotations |

There is no "lost" button. The app never uses that word.
