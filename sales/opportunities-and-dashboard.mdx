## Opportunities tab

Shows appointments and leads linked to your account.

- Tap a row for customer contact details and address
- Start or resume the solar workflow from the opportunity detail screen
- Appointments synced from your CRM (GoHighLevel) appear here automatically

If an expected appointment is missing, contact operations — do not create duplicate records unless you are trained on manual opportunity creation.

## Progress tab

Lists **active workflows** across all your in-progress jobs.

Use the search bar to filter by:

- Customer name
- Postcode or address
- Current stage name
- Status

Each row shows progress percentage and the current step title. Tap to open the workflow.

## Dashboard

Your personal performance view for the **current month**:

| Metric | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Appointments** | Count of appointments in the period |
| **Sales won** | Jobs marked **Won** |
| **Conversion rate** | Won ÷ appointments (percentage) |

Trend arrows compare to the previous month when data is available.

Outcome tracking applies to **Won** and **Quoted** results recorded after tracking was enabled — the dashboard may show a notice if historical data is incomplete.

## Statistics & analytics

From **Profile → Statistics & Analytics**, open deeper reports including won/quoted breakdowns and appointment cycle times (where your role has access).

## Finish appointment

When the visit ends, use **Finish appointment** from the workflow to record one of two outcomes:

| Outcome | When to use |
|---------|-------------|
| **Won** | Customer committed — contract signed or proceeding to install |
| **Quoted** | Customer received a quote but has not committed yet |

The app labels the quote outcome as **Quote** on some screens and **Quoted** on reports — they mean the same thing. There is no "lost" option.

Accurate outcomes keep dashboard and management reports correct.
