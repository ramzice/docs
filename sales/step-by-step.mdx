After you tap **Start Solar Progress**, work through these steps on the **Solar Progress** screen. Tap a step to open it, complete it, then return to Solar Progress for the next one.

## 1. Survey

**Survey** opens first.

- Most customer details are pre-filled — **double-check everything**
- Complete all required fields
- Add every required photo — installers and scaffolders use these on site
- Photos must be **clear and accurate** (roof, fuse board, access, shading, etc.)
- Save as you go; submit when complete

<Note>
If the customer **does not have an energy bill**, say so in the survey. An **Energy Bill Disclaimer** signing step may appear later in the workflow.
</Note>

## 2. OpenSolar — link your design

After the survey, open **OpenSolar** from Solar Progress.

Before the appointment, have designs ready in OpenSolar. During the visit you can:

1. **Search by address** — find an existing project
2. **Enter the Project ID** — from the Manage tab in OpenSolar
3. **Create a design** — ideally completed the day before the appointment

After linking:

- Confirm the design is saved
- Tap **Complete Step** if shown
- Return to the app (**Solar Progress**)

## 3. Calculate — choose calculator type

Tap **Calculate**. You choose:

| Calculator | When to use |
|------------|-------------|
| **Off Peak Calculator** | Customer has day & night rate, an EV charger, or low usage |
| **Flux Calculator** | Single rate or Octopus Flux customers with higher usage |

Then complete:

- Template selection (system options)
- Customer details if prompted
- Calculator inputs (arrays, battery, consumption)
- **System Pricing** — review figures before presenting

<Warning>
Present pricing only after you are happy the calculator is correct. See [In-app tools](/sales/in-app-tools) to change price if needed.
</Warning>

## 4. Proposal

Tap **Proposal** on Solar Progress.

- Generate and review the proposal
- Present it to the customer in the appointment

## 5. Solar Projection

Tap **Solar Projection**.

- Walk the customer through projected savings and performance

## 6. Hometree

Tap **Hometree** on Solar Progress.

- The app opens the Hometree Finance login page in your browser
- The step marks complete when opened — you can return to Hometree anytime

<Note>
When the customer wants to proceed on HT, follow the full [Hometree process](/sales/hometree) — including Teams updates and when to tap **Won**.
</Note>

## 7. Contract generation

Tap **Contract Generation** (**Select Contract Sheet**).

- Choose the correct proposal sheet (Off Peak or Flux/EPVS as shown)
- Tap **Generate Contract**
- Review the PDF

Generate the **EPVS contract** before filling Hometree — contract details feed into the HT application.

## 8. Energy Bill Disclaimer (if required)

Only appears when the survey recorded **no energy bill**.

- Send and complete the disclaimer signing
- Wait for signed status before continuing

## 9. Contract signing

Tap **Contract Signing**.

- Have the customer sign the contract in-app or via the signing link
- Confirm signed status before moving on

## 10. Express consent

Tap **Express Consent Signing**.

- Send and complete express consent with the customer

## 11. Booking confirmation

Tap **Booking Confirmation Signing**.

- Complete booking confirmation signing with the customer

## 12. Payment

Tap **Payment** (**Process Payment**).

1. Open your **Lopay** app on your phone
2. Take the **£500 holding payment** from the customer
3. Back in the app, tap **Mark Payment Complete & Continue**

<Warning>
Only mark payment complete after the £500 has successfully gone through on Lopay.
</Warning>

## 13. Book installation

Tap **Book Installation**.

- **Select an installer** from the list shown for your account
- Pick a date **at least two weeks** ahead
- Select **09:00** in available times (required for the booking to work)
- Tap **Book Installation**
- Confirm the success message, then **Continue to Next Step**

Some installers need two days on the calendar — follow the on-screen installer notes.

## 14. Welcome email and finish

Tap **Send Welcome Email**.

- Check customer name, email, and address
- Tap **Send Welcome Email**
- The app moves you to **Finish Appointment**

### Mark Won or Quote

On **Finish Appointment** choose:

| Option | When |
|--------|------|
| **Won** | Customer accepted — files go to **Customer Orders 2** |
| **Quote** | Customer took a quote only — files go to **Customer Quotations** |

Tap **Confirm & Organize Files**.

You can also tap **Won** or **Quote** earlier on the **Solar Progress** header when the customer decides — see [Hometree process](/sales/hometree) for when to mark **Won** during HT.

## Step order on Solar Progress

Your screen may show these labels (plus Energy Bill Disclaimer when needed):

1. Survey  
2. OpenSolar  
3. Calculate  
4. Proposal  
5. Solar Projection  
6. Hometree  
7. Contract Generation  
8. Energy Bill Disclaimer *(conditional)*  
9. Contract Signing  
10. Express Consent Signing  
11. Booking Confirmation Signing  
12. Payment  
13. Book Installation  
14. Send Welcome Email  

Then **Finish Appointment** for **Won** or **Quote**.
