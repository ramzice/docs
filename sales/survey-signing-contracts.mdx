## Site survey

The survey captures everything installers and compliance need. Required items typically include:

### Photos

- Full property (front and relevant elevations)
- All roof sections where panels will go
- Fuse board and nearby equipment
- Access routes (internal and external)
- Shading objects (trees, dormers, chimneys)
- EV charger location (if applicable)

Each photo field shows helper text describing what to capture. Minimum photo counts apply on some sections — you cannot submit until they are met.

### Property & energy data

- Address and occupancy details
- Annual consumption (from bill or calculated from spend)
- Whether the customer has an energy bill
- Installation notes and constraints

### Autosave

Survey data autosaves while you work. Use **Save** or **Submit** when a section is complete. If you change **Has energy bill** from Yes to No, return to the workflow — a disclaimer signing step may appear.

## Signing steps

Signing uses integrated providers (e.g. DocuSeal). Each step shows status: pending, sent, or signed.

### Energy bill disclaimer

Required when the customer cannot provide a bill. Send the document and wait for signed status before continuing.

### Express consent

Early consent for contact and data processing where your process requires it.

### Contract generation

Select the correct contract template and version. Generate the PDF, review it with the customer, then send for signature.

### Contract signing

Open the signing link on a tablet for the customer. Confirm all parties have signed before marking the step complete.

### Booking & email confirmation

Final confirmations after the customer commits — follow on-screen prompts.

<Warning>
Do not skip signing steps. Downstream installation and compliance checks depend on completed documents.
</Warning>

## If signing fails

1. Refresh the workflow screen and check status again
2. Try **Open in browser** if in-app signing does not load
3. Confirm the customer email and phone on the opportunity are correct
4. Contact support with the opportunity ID if the document stays stuck in pending

## Contract PDFs

Generated contracts can be previewed in-app. Use **Open in browser** to download or share only through approved channels — never send unsigned drafts to customers via personal email.
