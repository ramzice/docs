## Cannot log in

- Confirm you are on [app.creativuk.co.uk](https://app.creativuk.co.uk) (not a bookmarked old URL)
- Try **Forgot password** and check spam for reset emails
- If login succeeds but immediately logs you out, clear browser cache or try a private window
- Contact your manager if the account is locked or inactive

## App is slow or will not load

- Check Wi‑Fi or mobile data at the property
- Refresh the page (web) or force-close and reopen the app
- Avoid running heavy downloads on the same connection during photo uploads

## Survey photos will not upload

- Grant camera and photo library permissions when prompted
- Use good lighting and keep file size reasonable — very large images may timeout
- Save the section and retry individual fields
- On web, ensure the browser allows camera access (HTTPS required)

## Workflow step stuck or missing

- Pull to refresh on the Solar workflow screen
- Complete the previous step fully (including submit, not just save)
- If you changed survey answers (e.g. energy bill), go back to workflow — new steps may appear
- Admins can reset steps — ask operations if you are blocked

## Signing link does not open

- Tap **Open in browser** from the signing screen
- Disable pop-up blockers for app.creativuk.co.uk
- Verify customer email on the opportunity record
- Wait 1–2 minutes and refresh status — webhooks can delay updates

## Calculator or pricing errors

- Fill all required fields (look for validation messages)
- All appointments use the **Off-peak** calculator — if the calculator step does not load, refresh and try again
- Note the opportunity ID and contact operations for override or template issues

## API or network errors

Short network failures may show generic error messages. Retry once. If errors persist:

1. Note the time and what you were doing
2. Copy the opportunity ID from the URL or workflow screen
3. Contact support or your team lead

Developers: see the frontend repo `docs/TROUBLESHOOTING.md` for IIS, routing, and API URL issues.

## Who to contact

| Issue | Contact |
|-------|---------|
| Account access | Your manager or IT |
| Missing appointment in app | Operations / CRM admin |
| Pricing override or template | Operations |
| Signing stuck after 30+ minutes | Support with opportunity ID |
| Bug or app crash | Support with steps to reproduce |

## Still stuck?

Open **Profile → Support & Help** to return to this knowledge base, or email [support@creativuk.co.uk](mailto:support@creativuk.co.uk).
