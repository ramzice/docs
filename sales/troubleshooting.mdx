title: "Troubleshooting"
description: "Fix common problems during appointments in the app."
---

## Cannot log in

- Use [app.creativuk.co.uk](https://app.creativuk.co.uk) — not an old bookmark
- Check your username and password carefully (shared with you privately)
- Clear browser cache or use a private window if you log out immediately
- **There is no forgot-password option in the app** — contact your team lead if you cannot log in or need your password reset

## Appointment not in the list

- Expand **Appointment Date Filter** on **Appointments** and tap **This Week** or **This Month** — the app shows **today only** by default, so future appointments are hidden until you change the filter
- Tap the refresh icon at the top of **Appointments**
- Check **Next Week** or **Next Month** if the visit is further ahead
- If it still does not appear after checking the filter, contact your team lead — do not create a duplicate job unless trained to

## Solar Progress step greyed out

- Finish the previous step fully (submit, not just save)
- On **OpenSolar**, link a project ID — the step counts complete when linked
- If **Energy Bill Disclaimer** should appear, save the survey with "no energy bill" and refresh Solar Progress
- Ask your team lead if a step stays blocked

## Survey photos will not upload

- Allow camera and photo access in browser or device settings
- Use good lighting; retry one photo at a time
- Save the section before moving on

## OpenSolar will not link

- Search by full address first
- Or paste the **Project ID** from OpenSolar Manage tab
- Open the design URL to confirm it is the right property
- Tap **Complete Step** after linking

## Wrong calculator or price

- **Calculate** → pick **Off Peak** or **Flux** using the rules in [Step-by-step](/sales/step-by-step#3-calculate--choose-calculator-type)
- To change price: **Tools** (wrench) → **Override a Price** — see [In-app tools](/sales/in-app-tools)
- To start over: **Reset Progress** (only if your team lead agrees)

## Signing stuck on pending

- Refresh Solar Progress and check status again
- Use **Open in browser** if signing does not load in-app
- Confirm customer email on Opportunity Details
- Wait 1–2 minutes — status can lag briefly

## Payment step

- Take **£500** on **Lopay** first
- Only then tap **Mark Payment Complete & Continue** in the app
- If the button fails, note the opportunity and contact support

## Installation booking

- Date must be **at least two weeks** ahead
- You must select **09:00** in available times
- Pick an installer from your list — if none show, contact your team lead

## Hometree / HT issues

- Follow [Hometree process](/sales/hometree) — post in Teams and wait for office approval
- Do not skip contract generation before filling HT
- Screenshot must show Panel, Inverter, Battery, and Generation boxes

## App slow or frozen

- Check Wi‑Fi or mobile data
- Refresh the page (web) or close and reopen the app
- Avoid large downloads on the same connection while uploading survey photos

## Still stuck?

Contact your team lead with:

- Customer name and address  
- Which step you are on (exact label from Solar Progress)  
- A screenshot if possible  

Email [support@creativuk.co.uk](mailto:support@creativuk.co.uk) for app errors that block the appointment.
